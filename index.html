Overview

I am a fifth-year PhD candidate at UC Davis- Agricultural Economics with special interest in agricultural and development economics, with a touch of applied econometrics. My job market paper is on Supply response at the field level: disentangling area and yield effects. I use big data techniques, namely remote sensing of satellite data, to assemble a unique dataset of field-level yields for corn and soybeans in the US Midwest.
